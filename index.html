Owner

Carol Creamer, CPDT-KA, CTDI, CCC, CCFT, FPMT

Carol has been working with animals for almost 20 years. Her experience includes working as an animal shelter volunteer, foster parent of many dogs and cats from various animal shelters and rescue groups, pet store animal caretaker, zookeeper, veterinary assistant, veterinary technician, animal cruelty investigator, and dog trainer.Carol has also attended many continued education events on various topics related to veterinary medicine, dog training, dog behavior, & pet nutrition.
